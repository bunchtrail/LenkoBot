# Code of Conduct

LenkoBot contributors, maintainers, and community participants are expected to
keep collaboration technically rigorous, welcoming, and safe.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and code without attacking the person presenting them.
- Give specific, actionable feedback and accept correction in good faith.
- Respect different experience levels, backgrounds, languages, and viewpoints.
- Keep private reports, credentials, and personal data confidential.
- Take responsibility for mistakes and help repair their impact.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, or personal insults.
- Publishing another person's private information without explicit permission.
- Deliberately exposing credentials, vulnerabilities, or personal conversation
  data.
- Sustained disruption, spam, or bad-faith manipulation of project processes.
- Retaliation against someone who reports a concern in good faith.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions,
and private project communication. It also applies when someone represents the
project in another public space.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer using the email address
published on [@bunchtrail's GitHub profile](https://github.com/bunchtrail).
Do not include unrelated personal data. Security vulnerabilities should instead
follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

The maintainer will review available evidence, protect reporter confidentiality
where practical, and choose a proportionate response. Responses may include a
private warning, removal of content, temporary participation limits, or a ban.
The maintainer must disclose and recuse from a conflict of interest when another
neutral reviewer is available.
